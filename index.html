Your Favorites! The Golden Apple is bestowed upon those hotels that have earned exceptional ratings on our Vacation Satisfaction Questionnaires, completed by Apple Vacationers upon their return home. Some Golden Apple hotels are moderate and other deluxe, but all offer an exceptional standard of quality, service and value.
